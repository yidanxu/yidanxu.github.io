Yidan Xu Biography

About me:

Yidan Xu (also called Sofia) was born in Dalian, China. She moved to the USA to attend high school. She graduated from Cornell University with a degree in government, but later discovered a love for programming.

She is now enrolled in the computer science post-baccalaureate program at Tufts University. She hopes to continue her career in programming. Her hobbies are watching anime and Lord of the Rings and learning Japanese.
